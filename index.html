List of Main Characters

This website shows simple descriptions of four main characters (with an example picture) for an upcoming story I want to create one day. Title of story is undecided.

William Morozov
A white-haired, red-eyed demon who possess a great amount of fighting strength. He comes of stoic and intimidating, but deep down he has a heart of gold.
Maya Stoicheio
A half-wolf-half-human creature with brown hair and yellow eyes. Her adventerous attuitude and encouragment always brings a smile to people's faces.
Felice Occhiali
Another demon with red hair and green eyes. His happy-go-lucky personality and determination makes him well-liked amoungst his friends and peers.
Sapphire Austere
A human with black and white hair and deep blue eyes. She is an exotic character, known for her steadfastness and impulsive behavior.
